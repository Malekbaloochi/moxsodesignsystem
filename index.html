    Why this matters

    Most breaches start with a person, not a system.

    Traditional security awareness training is built around compliance — annual check-boxes, generic videos, one-size-fits-all campaigns. It doesn't change behaviour. It doesn't scale to individual risk. And it can't prove outcomes.

    Moxso replaces that model entirely. Every employee gets a risk profile. Every intervention is automated and adaptive. Every outcome is measurable — for regulators, leadership, and your team.

    74% of breaches involve humans

    Phishing, credential theft, and social engineering remain the top entry points for attackers. Technical defences alone aren't enough.

    Annual training doesn't stick

    Studies show employees forget 50–80% of security training within a week. Behaviour change requires continuous, personalised engagement.

    Regulators want proof

    NIS2, GDPR, and ISO 27001 require documented evidence of human risk reduction — not just training records. Moxso generates that automatically.

    The Moxso engine

    One continuous system. Three coordinated layers.

    Step 01 — Simulate

    Measure where your people are today.

    Run realistic phishing simulations — standard, risk-based, or spear-targeted — to establish a baseline. See exactly which teams and roles are most exposed, without guesswork.

    • Automated phishing campaigns by role or team
    • Real-time click rate and vulnerability data
    • Multi-channel attack simulations (Adapt & Defend)
    Step 02 — Train

    Deliver training that actually changes behaviour.

    No more generic videos. Moxso builds an individual risk profile for each employee and automatically assigns the right training at the right moment — delivered via Slack, Teams, or email.

    • Adaptive learning paths per employee
    • Multilingual content across 30+ languages
    • Policy management and bring-your-own content
    Step 03 — Measure

    Prove it's working — to regulators and leadership.

    Moxso tracks resilience scores across your organisation over time. You get a clear, auditable picture of risk reduction — ready for compliance reports, board meetings, and NIS2 documentation.

    • Individual and organisational resilience scores
    • Industry benchmarks and trend analytics
    • Organisational reporting for audits and leadership
